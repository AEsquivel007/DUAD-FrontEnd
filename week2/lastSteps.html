Voting process:

Thank you for registering to vote! Your registration has been successfully submitted.
You can now proceed to the next steps in the voting process. Please follow the instructions below:

  • Check your email for a confirmation of your registration.
  • Visit your local election office to verify your registration details.
  • Review the voting guidelines and requirements in your area.
  • Prepare for the upcoming election by researching candidates and measures.
  • Make sure to vote on election day or during the early voting period.
